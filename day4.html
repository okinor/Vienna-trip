Schedule Day 4 - Sunday December 23rd

Time Activity Description Address Map Links Links & Info
0900-0930 Breakfast Start your morning with building up energy for an exciting day!
0930-1000 Rails to start our day Follow the path on the map link to start our last full day. Hotel to Michaelerplatz
1000-1100 Our way to Michaelerplatz As you saw in the map link above, the walk between the station and Michaelerplatz is going through Volksgarten street, which is surrounded by beautiful places such as Heldenplatz, Volksgarten club and all kind of grassy terrains and bronze statues. Feel free to stray off the path and explore this beautiful place.
1100-1200 Hop on Welcome to the one of a kind, extraordinary horse show! There's no better way to start our last day with this true Europian expirience, so sit back and enjoy the show! Michaelerplatz 1, 1010 Wein
1200-1630 A civilized ending Another European classics are museums of course, so continuouesly to our show, we are about to absorb some culture visiting Vienna's Nature's History Museum. Take a look of nature's finest during this next few hours, watching the evolution of species and resources over the years in the exhibitions, shows etc. Make sure you grab your lunch in one of the museum's food facilities. Burgring 7, 1010 Wien Michaelerplatz to Museum To visit the museum website Click Here
1630-1830 The last nap Go back to the hotel and take your final nap for this trip seriously before we head back to the city once again. You can also start you packing if you can, it will save you some time for later. Museum to Hotel
1830-1900 A new place awaits We are about to go to our last christmas expirience for this trip, so put your smile on and ring those jingle bells! Hotel to Prater Park
1900-2200 Winter is coming Welcome to Prater's Winter market! This place is full with great expiriences for your last night out, including live music shows, food stands, the chair-o-planes, ferris wheel and much more! party out your last night because winter is already here! For more informatin about the winter market Click Here
2200-2300 The final countdown After we seased our last night out, it's time for our final rail ride. Once you get to the hotel make sure you're all packed up for tomorrow. Early wake-up call awaits! Prater park to Hotel
