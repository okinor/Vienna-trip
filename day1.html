Schedule Day 1 - Thursday December 20th

Time Activity Description Address Map Links Links & Info
1900-1930 Landing Landing at Vienna's airport and baggage claim.
1930-2000 Driving to hotel 1. After baggage claim, follow the signs inside the airport to the taxi station near the airport exit.
2. Once you get into a cab, ask the driver to take you to 'Linder hotel am belvedere'. It should be about 20 minutes ride. 		Airport to Hotel
2000-2030 Arriving to hotel and check in When you arrive to the hotel, go to the reception counter and check in your reservation. Afterwards, go up to your room to set your luggage and settle for a bit. Rennweg 12, 1030 Wien For more info about the hotel Click Here
2030-0000 Christmas market and dinner time After you put your luggage in your room you're now up for 2 activities:
1. Christmas market at belvedere palace – walk around and absorb the magic of Christmas time on the background of the breath-taking palace.
2. Dinner at 'Salm Braeu' restaurant – after a long journey, kick back and enjoy your first Austrian culinary experience.
Both of the above are a few minutes walking distance, so the order is up to you, depends on your mood and hunger level. The restaurant is open until midnight so the choice is yours. Feel free to go back to the hotel the same path any time you'd like. 		Address 'Salm Braeu': Rennweg 8, 1030 Wien. Hotel to Belvedere palace
