So how's this works?

On the toolbar above you can see the days bars. Once you click on a day, you'll reach to the full schedule of it. Each schedule is devided into six columns:

  • Time - Shows the time range of the current activity.
  • Activity - A fun title describing our next attraction.
  • Description - Detailed information about the upcoming activity.
  • Address - Lists the Address of the activity's location.
  • Map links - Shows the exact way to our destination from our current location using 'Google Maps'.
  • Links & Info - Presents links to external resources about the activity if you want to get extra information.

In addition to the days pages, don't forget to check the tips page that includes really valuable tips for your upcoming trip.

Before we dive in...

Few things you should know:

  1. All timings in the schedule are synced with the opening hours of the different facilities.
  2. All map links will open up on 'Google maps', so if you're surfing from your phone it's recommended to have the app installed.
  3. Some of the external websites will open automatically in German. That's ok, just find the change language button and choose English.
